Trades Offered

Electrician

Duration: 2 years (4 semesters) Seats Available: 20 Details: The Electrician trade involves training in electrical systems, wiring, insulation, domestic and industrial installations, circuit breakers, and safety protocols. Electricians are in demand for both residential and commercial work.

Fitter

Duration: 2 years (4 semesters) Seats Available: 20 Details: The Fitter trade includes working with machines and their components, assembling, cutting, filing, and metalworking techniques. It is essential in the mechanical and manufacturing industries.

Mechanic Motor Vehicle

Duration: 2 years Seats Available: 48 Details: This course teaches the repair and maintenance of motor vehicles, including engine overhauling, brake systems, transmission, and vehicle diagnostics. It's ideal for those interested in working in automobile workshops or service centers.

Turner

Duration: 2 years Seats Available: 20 Details: The Turner trade involves shaping metal parts using a lathe machine. Training includes precision machining, drilling, and cutting operations. It plays a key role in the machine tool and manufacturing sectors.

Wireman

Duration: 2 years Seats Available: 40 Details: In this trade, students learn about electrical wiring, fault detection, installation, and maintenance of electrical systems. Wiremen are vital in domestic and industrial electrical setups.

Draughtsman (Mechanical)

Duration: 2 years Seats Available: 20 Details: This trade focuses on preparing technical drawings using instruments and CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software. Students learn to design mechanical parts and industrial layouts. It's useful in design and planning roles.

About Us

Sant Muktabai Govt.Tribal Industrial Training Institute, Jondhankheda, is a premier vocational training center located in Jalgaon district, Maharashtra. Established with the vision of empowering youth with technical skills and hands-on industrial training, our institute plays a pivotal role in bridging the gap between education and employment.

We offer a wide range of NCVT/SCVT-affiliated trades including Electrician, Fitter, Mechanic Motor Vehicle, Turner, Wireman, Draughtsman Mechanical, and more. Our courses are designed to equip students with job-ready skills through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical training.
